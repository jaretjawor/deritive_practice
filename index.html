Derivative Practice Sheet

Find \( \frac{d}{dx} \left( x^4 - 3x^2 + 7x - 5 \right) \)
Answer: \( 4x^3 - 6x + 7 \)
Find \( \frac{d}{dx} \left( \frac{2x^3 + 5x}{x^2 - 1} \right) \)
Answer: \( \frac{(6x^2 + 5)(x^2 - 1) - (2x^3 + 5x)(2x)}{(x^2 - 1)^2} \)
Find \( \frac{d}{dx} \left( \arcsin(x^2) \right) \)
Answer: \( \frac{2x}{\sqrt{1 - x^4}} \)
Find \( \frac{d}{dx} \left( e^{x^2} \sin(x) \right) \)
Answer: \( e^{x^2} \left( 2x \sin(x) + \cos(x) \right) \)
Find \( \frac{d}{dx} \left( \ln(x^2 + 1) \right) \)
Answer: \( \frac{2x}{x^2 + 1} \)
Find \( \frac{d}{dx} \left( \tan^{-1}(x^3) \right) \)
Answer: \( \frac{3x^2}{1 + x^6} \)
Find \( \frac{d}{dx} \left( \frac{e^x}{x^2 + 1} \right) \)
Answer: \( \frac{e^x(x^2 + 1) - 2x e^x}{(x^2 + 1)^2} \)
Find \( \frac{d}{dx} \left( x^2 \ln(x) \right) \)
Answer: \( 2x \ln(x) + x \)
Find \( \frac{d}{dx} \left( \frac{1}{x^2 + 4} \right) \)
Answer: \( -\frac{2x}{(x^2 + 4)^2} \)
Find \( \frac{d}{dx} \left( \sin(x) \arccos(x) \right) \)
Answer: \( \cos(x) \arccos(x) - \frac{\sin^2(x)}{\sqrt{1 - x^2}} \)
